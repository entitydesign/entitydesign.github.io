Arktos
Design

Arktos is a Front End Developer and UX Designer in Bristol, UK. With the name deriving from the Greek term for Bears, a strong, stand-out brand was the main focus of his style amongst a competitive industry.

Bear
necessities

Arktos required a minimal brand style accompanied by a vectorized logo that would lend itself nicely towards JavaScript web animation. As the branding was completely new, Arktos also required a colour palette and web-safe typography in order to kick start his web design showcase.

Vectors
& motion

Creating 2 versions of the logo opened up infinitely more options for web animations and transitions. One logo to reflect a minimal style, and the other more bright and prominent. With the use of simple circular and rounded shapes, Arktos was able to manipulate every element of the logo and bring it to life.
