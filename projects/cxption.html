Cxption

Cxption at its most basic form is a film and photography platform that aims to showcase and connect the innovative and independent artists of the UK and Ireland. Cxption is a collective of artists that explores health, culture and the raw beauty of being. All coming from different walks of life; nations, cultures, and societies.

Simplistic and Scalable

Cxption were looking for a logo that aligned with the core principles of growth, independence and creativity; a logo which represents these values and has the power to captivate its audience. The logo’s primary requirement was to look simplistic and scalable due to the magazine being an online platform, the logo needed to be clearly visible at favicon size.

Flexible and
Future-proof

Taking inspiration from a targeted lens of a film camera; Cxption’s logo is a snapshot graphic focussing on perspective and space as well as representing each person’s story accurately. The logo type is designed to complement the logo icon with its blocky and urban design. Cxption has the option of using the logo type separately to the logo icon as it revolves around written content as much as visual media; in keeping with the magazines flexible and forward-thinking nature.
